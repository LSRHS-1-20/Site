The Elements of Citizenship

1. Honesty is when one always tells the truth. It is important because one who has this quality can be trusted.

2. Compassion is when one has sympathy towards others. It's key because nobody likes a stone cold being with no empathy.

3. Respect is when one has admiration for another. It is essential to have respect for someone else because then you have a role model.

4. Responsibility is when one has authority over certain people or actions. Being responsible shows that one is capable and a leader.

5. Courage is when one is brave. This shows that one is fearless, confident, and capable.

Another essential qualituy of a good citizen is STRENGTH.This is when one has the willpower and discipline to stand tall in tough situations. This is key because sometimes other citizens may criticize you for who you are but you must have the strength to remain true to yourself.

A great act of citizenship that I've heard about is a time where a homeless man discovered a bag of $50,000, but instead of taking it, he returned it to the police station. One would expect him to take it since he really needed it but he had the strength and respect to return the money to its rightful owner. He ended up being rewarded some money for his act of responsibility and discipline.
